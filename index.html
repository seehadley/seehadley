Project 1 of 3

Indeed.jobs

Indeed was about to launch its first ever brand campaign, with commercials in multiple countries. The existing indeed.jobs site had existed basically unchanged since it was launched in 2006. With increased awareness of Indeed and potential traffic to indeed.jobs, the brand marketing team wanted a fresh design for a responsive site.

Role: design and develop

Launched: October 2014

Art direction: J Christopher Garcia and Bianca Brigandi

Old and not responsive Indeed.jobs

The brand team explicitly requested a hero image and landing pages for each job category and location.

We went through a few iterations of wireframes. These are the ones that got approved.

To help give the site a unique personality, I suggested photographs of Indeed employees for the home page and department pages and helped to art direct the shoot. I spent a lot of time combing stock photography sites for the location shots.

Redesigned Indeed.jobs

The results of the redesign were positive, with an increase in session duration and decrease in bounce rate.

The site was redesigned again in July 2016 when Indeed moved to a new system for hosting jobs. You can browse the one I made here.

Project 2 of 3

Joshua Mark Phillippe

Joshua Mark Phillippe is an artist from Indianapolis. His portfolio was my first responsive site, built in 2012. It needed some performance enhancements and was ready for a fresh visual design.

Role: design and develop

Launched: October 2016

Old joshuamark.net. Not terrible, but slow and somewhat dull.

WebPagetest results for the old site. This is not good.

The grayscale and cropped treatment was intended to provide a reveal, with color on hover and full image on click. With the redesign, I removed the extra steps and placed full-color, accurately scaled thumbnails on the homepage.

Of course, with the site's focus on imagery, I had to balance quality with page weight and speed. To do this, I focused on image optimization and am only loading images in view by default. The rest are lazy loaded. The result was a drop of about 77% in page load time.

New joshuamark.net

Down from ~14.4s load time to ~3.3s, with color and everything.

Without much organic traffic to Joshua's site, my metrics for success were improved performance and client satisfaction. Thumbs up all around.
